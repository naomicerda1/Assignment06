My Recommendation for HORROR Movies

1935: Bride of Frankenstein Synopsis

Not Rated / Length: 1 Hour & 15 Minutes

Drama, Horror, Sci-Fi

This movie is a sequel to 1931: Frankenstein.
It all began with Dr. Frankenstein's obsession with creating a human life with other human parts that were pronounced dead. His helper, Igor, goes through various brains to find the right one and at last, he steals a brain that is being studied at a university. After his creation, Frankenstein, tormented his village and was "killed", Frankenstein has come back but now he wants a mate! With the help of another mad scientist, Dr. Frankenstein and Dr. Pretorius create a woman monster, The Bride of Frankenstein!

2012: The Lords of Salem

Rated: R / Length: 1 Hour & 45 Minutes

Horror, Thriller

This Rob Zombie is one of the most confusing yet intriguing movie I have ever watched. It may be disturbing for some because of the content. Heidi is a radio host for Salem, Massachusetts. She is also a recovering addict who has a troubling past. The Lords, other wise known as the witches, of Salem are coming to take back their town a rid of the "filth" that has ruined their town. The Lords choose Heidi to be their head queen and as much as she tried to ignore the fact, The Lords take over and she's in for a long ride.

2004: Shaun of The Dead

Rated: R / Length: 1 Hour & 39 Minutes

Horror, Comedy

Simon Pegg and Edgar Wright are some of my favorite actors! They are the main characters that go by Shaun and Ed. Two best friends that live together in England with an old friend from college. One day Shaun wakes up after having an interesting night and suddenly he has to protect his friends and family from a zombie apocalypse! But in the mix of everything, he's trying to salvage is relationship with is ex and be friendly with his step-father who was bitten by a zombie.
